Frequently asked questions

The concerns we hear most -- and the honest answers.

How is AI feedback better than practicing with a colleague? ▾
Colleagues go easy on you. The AI doesn't. It raises the real objections you'll face, pushes back when your answer is weak, and gives you specific, actionable feedback -- not "that was good, try again."
Will I actually see improvement, or is this just "feel-good" practice? ▾
Every call is scored 1-10 with specific breakdowns: what you nailed, what you missed, and three things to fix. You track your scores over time. If you're not improving, you'll see it in the data.
What if the AI feedback isn't helpful or accurate? ▾
The AI analyzes your actual transcript against proven sales methodologies. It flags specific moments -- "you accepted the objection instead of challenging it" or "you pitched before asking a single question." It's specific to what you said, not generic advice.
What is Role Reversal Mode? ▾
After your call, you can watch the AI become the salesperson and pitch to you using your product. It demonstrates exactly how a pro would handle the objections you just struggled with. You see the technique, then you copy it.
What browsers work best? ▾
Chrome on desktop or mobile gives the best experience. The voice conversation works best in Chrome.
Who is this for? ▾
