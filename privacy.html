Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account data: Email address, account identifier, authentication and login session information, and password-reset or email-confirmation requests when you create or use an account.
  • Practice content: Product descriptions, scripts, benefits, objections, and call transcripts you submit or generate during practice sessions. These are processed to provide live roleplay, script tools, and coaching, and completed roleplay transcripts are not currently saved to customer accounts.
  • Technical data: Basic technical information needed to operate, troubleshoot, and secure the Service, such as browser, device, request, and security-related information.
  • Payment data: MyPitchGym does not currently operate production subscription billing or store production payment details.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics data such as page views and feature usage (if analytics are enabled).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service (script generation, roleplay, coaching)
  • To create and manage email-and-password accounts, login sessions, email confirmations, and password resets
  • To improve the Service and fix issues
  • To communicate with you about your account if necessary

3. Third-Party Services

The Service relies on the following third parties:

  • Supabase: Provides email-and-password authentication, login sessions, email confirmation, and password-reset functionality. Passwords are handled through Supabase authentication and are not stored directly in MyPitchGym frontend source code.
  • OpenAI: Processes information needed for live roleplay, coaching, and script tools, including practice content such as product info, scripts, objections, and transcripts. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosts the website and serverless functions. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Your subscription status is currently stored locally in your browser (localStorage). The new account foundation exists, but saved sessions, recordings, billing, and usage tracking are future features.

MyPitchGym does not currently save completed roleplay transcripts, call recordings, or session history to customer accounts. Practice content is processed through the services needed to provide the live experience and coaching.

5. Data Retention

Account authentication records are handled through Supabase. Completed roleplay transcripts, call recordings, and session history are not currently saved to customer accounts. This policy will need another update before transcripts, recordings, saved sessions, production billing, or usage tracking are enabled.

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. If we enable analytics in the future, we will use privacy-respecting tools and update this policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you through the contact method provided by MyPitchGym
  • Manage your login information through the account and password-reset flow
  • Clear your local data by clearing your browser's localStorage

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices. API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables on Vercel and are never exposed to the client. All API communication uses HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us through the contact method provided by MyPitchGym.
